History of BMW

The BMW company was founded on March 7, 1916, by ,Franz Josef Popp Karl Rapp, and Camillo Castiglioni. It originally produced aircraft engines.

The Best Sports Motorcycle

The BMW S1000RR 2023 is equipped with a 999cc inline-four engine and offers a top speed of 300 km/h. It is priced at around €25,000.

The Best Touring Motorcycle

The BMW K1600GTL features a 1649cc inline-six engine, delivering a comfortable ride with a top speed of 250 km/h. It is priced at about €23,000.
